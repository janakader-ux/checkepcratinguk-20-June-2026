Why bundle EPC, PAT and fire door together?

Each of these three services has a fixed on-site visit cost — travel, setup, time on-site. Booking them separately means paying that fixed cost three times. Bundling them into a single visit, with a single assessor, means you pay it once. That saving typically comes out at 15 – 20% compared with three separate bookings.

What's in the bundle

1. Domestic EPC — from £69

RdSAP-compliant Energy Performance Certificate, lodged on the official GOV.UK EPC Register, valid for 10 years. Required by law when selling or letting a property, and a standard HMO licence condition.

2. PAT testing — from £1.50 per item

Visual inspection, electrical test and pass/fail labelling of every portable appliance. Certificate and full itemised report issued the same day. Essential for landlords, HMO owners, offices and holiday-let insurers.

3. Fire door inspection — from £35 per door

BRE Academy 10-point inspection of every internal and flat-entrance fire door. Detailed photographic report identifying any defects and the priority order for repair.

Typical bundle for a 4-bedroom HMO in Bedford: EPC + ~25 items PAT + 5 fire doors → usually quoted at a single fixed fee. Ask us for an exact figure — we'll quote over the phone in under 5 minutes.

Who is the bundle for?

  • Landlords preparing a property to let — get everything done before the tenant moves in.
  • HMO owners renewing a licence — matches the documentation required by Bedford Borough, Central Bedfordshire, Milton Keynes, Luton and Hertfordshire licensing authorities.
  • Estate agents preparing a property for sale — a clean compliance pack removes friction from the buyer's conveyancing.
  • Portfolio landlords — we offer a further reduction for 3+ properties booked on consecutive visits.
  • New HMO licence applicants — one contact point for three of the documents you need.
  • Commercial landlords with mixed-use buildings — residential flats above shops, for example.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is in the EPC + PAT + Fire Door compliance bundle?

It is a single booked visit that produces three separate certificates: a domestic Energy Performance Certificate lodged on the EPC Register, a PAT testing certificate for all portable appliances, and a detailed photographic fire door inspection report. Certificates are usually issued the same working day.

How much can I save by bundling?

Booking all three services together typically saves 15 – 20% compared with booking each service separately, because the site-visit element is only charged once. Exact pricing depends on the size of the property and the number of appliances and doors.

Who is the bundle for?

Landlords preparing a property to let, HMO owners renewing a licence, estate agents preparing a property for sale with a clean compliance pack, and portfolio landlords wanting one assessor handling multiple compliance items in one go.

Do you cover more than Bedford?

Yes. We deliver the bundle across Bedford, Milton Keynes, Luton, Cambridge, St Albans, Stevenage, Aylesbury and most of Bedfordshire, Cambridgeshire, Buckinghamshire and Hertfordshire.
